Supported Banks

CSV Bank works with PDF statements from any bank that provides digital PDFs — which includes virtually every bank worldwide.

How does compatibility work?

CSV Bank reads the text directly from PDF files downloaded from your bank's online portal. Any bank that provides digital PDF statements is compatible — the format varies slightly between banks but the converter handles all common layouts automatically.

The only statements that may not work are those generated from scanned paper documents (image PDFs). If you downloaded your statement from online banking, it will almost certainly work.
